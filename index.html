About Us

Bestaf Payment Solutions Limited

Bestaf Payment provides products and services that enable business payments, improve efficiency, ease processes and meet our client expectations. We specialize in building platforms for electronic payments, funds disbursement, collections, and bill payments. We also work as a licensed Payment Terminal Service Provider to set up and manage Point of Sale (POS) terminals.
